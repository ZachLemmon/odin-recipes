Sour Cream Chip Muffins Recipe

Description

Take one bite and you'll see why I think these rich, tender muffins are the best I've ever tasted. Mint chocolate chips make them a big hit with my family and friends

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 5 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup mint or semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  2. Combine the egg, sour cream, butter and vanilla. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full
  4. Bake at 350Â° for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean
  5. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
